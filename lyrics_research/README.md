# Serbian/Bosnian Lyrics Research

Educational research project: analyze Serbian/Bosnian club/pop and hip-hop/drill hits to understand lyrical structure, themes, and poetic devices. Contains two 10-song datasets.

## Structure

- `data/candidates.csv` — candidate song shortlist with chart/source metadata (tech-house/pop).
- `data/dataset_10_songs.json` — final 10 tech-house/pop songs with lyrics and metadata.
- `data/corpus.txt` — clean lyrics text for training/prompting (tech-house/pop).
- `reports/analysis_report.md` — per-song close reading + aggregate findings (tech-house/pop).
- `reports/style_guide.md` — recurring patterns, hooks, vocabulary palette (tech-house/pop).
- `data/candidates_rap.csv` — candidate shortlist for hip-hop/drill/trap.
- `data/dataset_10_songs_rap.json` — final 10 hip-hop/drill songs with lyrics and metadata.
- `data/corpus_rap.txt` — clean lyrics text for training/prompting (hip-hop/drill).
- `reports/analysis_report_rap.md` — per-song close reading + aggregate findings (hip-hop/drill).
- `reports/style_guide_rap.md` — recurring patterns, hooks, vocabulary palette (hip-hop/drill).
- `templates/` — reusable lyric scaffolding in Serbian/Bosnian Latin script.
- `scripts/` — collection, cleaning, analysis, and dataset-build helpers.

## Copyright note
