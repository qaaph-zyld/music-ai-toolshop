# Template: Tech-House Hook (Serbian/Bosnian Electronic Track)

**Best for**: tech-house, electro pop, AI/modern-life concept tracks  
**Hook formula**: Identity chant + technology metaphor  
**English target**: 10–20% of tokens (tech terms and interjections)

## Verse 1 (8–10 lines, 5–7 tokens/line)
Establish a modern setting and a relationship mediated by technology or rhythm.

> [Digital object] me [verb]  
> [Screen / device] je [adjective]  
> [Action in app or game]  
> [Connection or disconnection]  
> [Feeling through technology]  
> [Specific jargon used as metaphor]  
> [A personal promise]

## Pre-chorus (2–4 lines, 4–6 tokens/line)
Turn the metaphor into a command or a question.

> [Tech command]  
> [Why do I / do you] [tech action]?  
> [Imperative] sada

## Chorus (4–6 lines, 3–5 tokens/line) — REPEAT 3×
The hook is a list of identity claims. Use short, punchy lines.

> [I am / We are] [X]  
> [Y] / [Z]  
> [Modern noun]  
> [Repetition of core concept]  
> [Romantic/aspirational line]

## Verse 2 (8–10 lines)
Deepen the metaphor. Add gaming, crypto, AI, or digital-culture specifics.

> [Platform / app / game]  
> [Multiplayer / singleplayer reference]  
> [Modern anxiety or fantasy]  
> [A regional twist on technology]  
> [Return to emotional core]

## Bridge (4 lines, stripped beat)
Repeat the central phrase with increasing intensity.

> [Core phrase]  
> [Core phrase + adjective]  
> [Core phrase + consequence]  
> [Final chant]

## Outro
Fade on the tech chant.

> [Tech word repeated]  
> [Tech word repeated]  
> [Fade]
