# Template: Balkan Pride Anthem (Serbian/Bosnian Pop/Club)

**Best for**: festival pop, club anthems, Eurovision-style tracks  
**Hook formula**: Identity chant + aspirational slogan  
**English target**: 10–20% of tokens (mostly interjections and hook sounds)

## Verse 1 (8–10 lines, 5–7 tokens/line)
Start with a problem or a question the chorus will answer.

> [What is missing / what is wrong]  
> [How people feel]  
> [A regional detail]  
> [Past glory or past sadness]  
> [The turning point]

## Pre-chorus (2–4 lines, 4–6 tokens/line)
Build collective energy. Use "we" and "us".

> [We have X]  
> [We have Y]  
> [Imperative] sada sve

## Chorus (4–6 lines, 3–5 tokens/line) — REPEAT 3×
Aspirational, chantable, with a repeated slogan.

> [Sound effect or chant syllable] — [geographic reach]  
> [Sound effect] — [group of people]  
> [Sound effect] — [who could object?]  
> [Aspirational line: our region/people will be X]  

## Verse 2 (8–10 lines)
Add specifics: music, food, dance, landscape, history.

> [Music detail]  
> [Cultural object]  
> [Action we do together]  
> [External judgment]  
> [Defiant response]  
> [Call to dance/celebrate]

## Bridge (4 lines, maximum lift)
Switch to English or a bilingual phrase for a festival moment.

> [English/bilingual phrase]  
> [Return to regional language]  
> [Imperative]  
> [Final chant]

## Outro
Pure chant.

> [Slogan]  
> [Slogan]  
> [Slogan, fading]
